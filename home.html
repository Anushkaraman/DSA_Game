Welcome to AlgoQuest

AlgoQuest is a dynamic and interactive game designed to help you master Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) in a fun and engaging way. Set in a virtual world with multiple villages, you’ll meet characters who will teach you complex algorithms, challenge you with quizzes, and reward you as you progress.

What You'll Learn

  • Data Structures (Arrays, Linked Lists, Trees, Graphs, etc.)
  • Sorting and Searching Algorithms
  • Dynamic Programming
  • Greedy Algorithms
  • And much more!

Explore AlgoQuest’s world while learning, practicing, and mastering DSA at your own pace.
